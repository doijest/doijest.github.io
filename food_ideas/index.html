Air Fryer: Cauliflower Pakoras

Ingredients

  • Plain White Flour 30g
  • 0% Fat Natural Yogurt 100g
  • Water 100ml
  • Paprika, level teaspoon(s) x 1
  • Chilli Powder, level teaspoon(s) x 1
  • Cauliflower, Raw 600g

Directions

  1. Mix everything but the cauliflower in a bowl using an electric whisk
  2. Cut the cauliflower into florets
  3. Dip half of the cauliflower in the mixture and then add to air fryer at 180 for 15 mins
  4. Take first batch out and store in microwave, then repeat a second batch

Air Fryer: Squash Diced

Ingredients

  • Butternut Squash x 1
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil, level tablespoon(s) x 1
  • Chilli Flakes, teaspoon(s), level x 1
  • Cumin Seeds, teaspoon(s), level x 1

Directions

  1. Set air fryer to 200 degrees
  2. Peel, deseed and dice squash, then add to basket
  3. Add the oil, chilli flakes and cumin seeds, then mix
  4. Cook for 30 mins, stirring every 10 mins

Air Fryer: Squash Quartered

Ingredients

  • Butternut Squash x 1
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil, level tablespoon(s) x 1
  • Chilli Flakes, teaspoon(s), level x 1
  • Cumin Seeds, teaspoon(s), level x 1

Directions

  1. Set air fryer to 200 degrees
  2. Peel, deseed and quarter squash
  3. Stack first 2 quarters in basket
  4. Sprinkle half the oil, chilli flakes and cumin seeds
  5. Stack second 2 quarters on top of first
  6. Sprinkle other half the oil, chilli flakes and cumin seeds
  7. Cook for 40 mins, swapping layers after 20 mins

Flat Bread

Ingredients

  • White Self Raising Flour 250g
  • 0% Fat Natural Yogurt 250g
  • Salt, pinch x 1
  • Baking powder, teaspoon(s), level x 1/2

Directions

  1. !!!This needs to be tried using the air fryer!!!
  2. Preheat fan oven to 200 degrees making sure 4 shelves are available
  3. Prepare 4 baking trays by placing greaseproof paper on each and then flouring the paper to prevent the dough sticking
  4. Add 250g of white self raising flour, baking powder and salt to a food processor and start processing, slowly add the natural yoghurt until it forms a dough, this should require all of the 250g
  5. Tip the dough out onto a floured chopping board and split into 4 balls, roll each ball out into a flat shape about the size of your baking trays, flouring the board and dough as necessary. Add each rolled out ball to a prepared baking tray and place in the oven for approx. 10 mins

Mushroom Stoganoff

Creamy and rich, this luxurious looking dish is surprisingly quick and easy to make. This veggie version is packed with flavour, with a little chilli powder giving it a hint of heat.

Ingredients

  • Calorie controlled cooking spray x 4 sprays
  • Onion(s), finely chopped x 1
  • Garlic, cloves, crushed x 2
  • Mushrooms 600g
  • Cannellini Beans, can(s), large, drained x 1
  • 0% Fat Natural Yogurt 30g
  • Philadelphia Lightest Soft Cheese 80g
  • Paprika, tablespoon(s), level x 1
  • Chilli Powder, teaspoon(s), level x 1
  • White or Brown Rice, as required

Directions

  1. Mist a large frying pan with cooking spray and cook the onion for 6-8 mins over a medium heat until soft, then add the spices and garlic and cook for another minute. Add the mushrooms and cook for 7-8 minutes, or until tender.
  2. Stir in the soft cheese and yogurt and allow to melt, then season to taste.
  3. Cook the rice to pack instructions and serve with the stroganoff.
